Selected Metalation Agents

A variety of metalation agents are used to deprotonate C-H acidic compounds. For materials with pK values above ca 37 only alkyllithium reagents are effective. For more acidic protons these may also work, but various lithium amides (especially LiNiPr2) are often faster and give cleaner products.
