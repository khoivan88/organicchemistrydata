Sonogashira Coupling

Sonogashira couplings use Pd(Ph3P)4 or Pd(Ph3P)2Cl2 catalysts with CuI as a co-catalyst to couple aryl or vinyl halides or triflates R-X to terminal alkynes in amine solvents. In these reactions the relatively acidic alkynyl-H is metalated by the copper salt, and then transmetalated in the usual way to Pd.
