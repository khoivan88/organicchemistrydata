Metalated Oxiranes

Oxiranyl Anions and Aziridinyl Anions,
  Satoh, T. Chem. Rev. 1996, 96, 3303-25.

Although epoxides are themselves quite reactive towards lithium reagents, it is posible to to metalate several types of functionalized epoxides, including those with sulphonyl, silyl, oxazoline and aryl substituents.
