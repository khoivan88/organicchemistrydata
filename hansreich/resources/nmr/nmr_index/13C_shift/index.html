  • Explanation

    For tables of data and substituent chemical shifts (Δδ) see Acetylene, Alkenes, Allene, Allyl, Benzene, Benzyl, Butyl, Cyciohexanes, Methyl, Naphthalene, Neopentane, Pentanes, Proparayl. Heterocycles are listed under Cycloalkanes, Heterocyles; Bicyclo[x.y.z]; as well as under specific names (e.g., Dithiane; Piperidine; Furan). A number in parentheses after a chemical shift is a coupling (e.g., J(C-Sn)).

    Chemical shifts marked with an asterisk (22.4*, 23.1*) have not been unambiguously assigned.

    An asterisk next to a reference (*ZOK-2) indicates that the paper has °C data for numerous other related compounds.
