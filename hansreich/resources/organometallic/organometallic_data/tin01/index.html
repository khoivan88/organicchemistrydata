Stannanes as Carbon Nucleophiles

Alkylstannanes are cleaved by strong electrophiles (e.g. Br2. Cl2), but are less useful for C-C bond formation because the reactivity is low, and there are problems with selectivity when there are multiple alkyl groups on the tin.
