Electrophilic allylsilanes

Allyl silanes with multiple electronegative substituents are often sufficiently Lewis acidic at silicon to react by cyclic mechanisms, as shown by the observation of different product stereochemistry for the E and Z crotyl trifluorosilanes. The reaction proceeds through pentacoordinate and even hexacoordinated Si intermediates, and may be subject to nucleophilic catalysis. Sakurai J. Am. Chem. Soc. 1989, 111, 6429.
