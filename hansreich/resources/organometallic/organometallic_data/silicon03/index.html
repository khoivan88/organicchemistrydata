Silicon-Based Protecting Groups

Use of Organosilicon Reagents as Protective Groups in Organic Synthesis,
  Lalonde, M.; Chan, T. H. Synthesis 1985, 817.
Silyl Ethers as Protective Groups for Alcohols: Oxidative Deprotection and Stability Under Alcohol Oxidation Conditions,
  Muzart, J. Synthesis 1993, 11.

Silicon-based protecting groups have the unique features that they can be cleaved under essentially neutral conditions with fluoride ion (typically BuN4F), and that their resistance to deprotection can be varied easily by varying the substituents on silicon.
