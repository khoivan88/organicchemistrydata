  In the Suzuki coupling an alkyl, vinyl, or aryl boron compound (usually a boronic acid or ester, RB(OH)2 or R-B(OR')2, but triaorganoboranes also work) is coupled with an electrophile, usually a vinyl or aryl halide or triflate. The organoborane is usually prepared by hydroboration of an alkyne or alkene.
